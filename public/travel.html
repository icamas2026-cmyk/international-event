Travel & Accommodation

Travel Information

Nearest Airport

Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, Guwahati (Approx. 400 km). Connected by flights from major Indian cities. Taxis and buses are available to reach NIT Arunachal Pradesh.

Nearest Railway Station

Naharlagun Railway Station (Approx. 30 km). Well connected to Guwahati and other parts of India. Taxis and local transport are available to reach the campus.

Bus Services

Regular bus services are available from Guwahati and Naharlagun to NIT Arunachal Pradesh. Comfortable overnight buses connect Guwahati to Itanagar/Naharlagun.

Accommodation

Hotel Blue Pine

Located near Naharlagun town. Comfortable rooms, restaurant service, and close proximity to NIT Arunachal.

Hotel Waii International

A popular choice with modern amenities and good transport access. Suitable for conference participants.

Hotel Pybss

Situated in Itanagar. Offers spacious rooms, WiFi, and dining facilities. Good option for delegates.

Hotel Rajhans

Located in Naharlagun, offering budget-friendly accommodation with clean rooms and basic amenities.
