Dr. Pulak Mohan Pandey

IHFC Chair & Professor (HAG), Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Delhi

Dr. Pulak Mohan Pandey is a distinguished academician and researcher in Additive Manufacturing, CAD/CAM, Biomedical Applications of 3D Printing, and Non-traditional Machining. He has served as the Director of BIET Jhansi (2021–2023) and has been a Professor at IIT Delhi since 2004. With a Ph.D. from IIT Kanpur, his contributions include pioneering research, patents, and impactful projects in advanced manufacturing.

Recognized with multiple Gandhian Young Technological Innovation Awards, the Outstanding Research Award (AMSI 2015), and the Distinguished Alumni Award (HBTU Kanpur), he continues to inspire students and researchers worldwide.

Dr. Tanmoy Mukhopadhyay

Senior Lecturer, Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics,
University of Southampton, UK
T.Mukhopadhyay@soton.ac.uk

Dr. Mukhopadhyay leads research in mechanical metamaterials, multi-functional composites, 2D materials, deployable structures, digital twins, and computational additive manufacturing. His expertise spans multi-scale and nano-mechanics, stochastic analysis, uncertainty quantification, optimization, machine learning, and AI applications in structural mechanics.

His Programmable Matter Lab provides opportunities for highly motivated students and researchers across Aerospace, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. Openings include PhD positions, postdoctoral fellowships, research internships, and thesis projects, fostering innovation at the intersection of materials, computation, and intelligent systems.
