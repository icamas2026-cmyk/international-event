Keynote Speakers

Prof. Sarit Kumar Das

Institute Professor & V. Balakrishnan Chair Professor,
Department of Mechanical Engineering,
IIT Madras, India

Prof. Sarit K. Das is a distinguished researcher in thermo-fluidics and nanofluids, with contributions in heat and mass transfer, fuel cells, battery cooling, and bio-microfluidics. His group is internationally known for pioneering work on nanofluids and biochips for medical diagnostics. He has also advanced desalination technologies, including HDH and flow electrode capacitive deionization.

A former Director of IIT Ropar and Dean of Academic Research at IIT Madras, Prof. Das has held visiting positions at MIT, USA and several European universities. He has authored 500+ publications with over 28,000 citations, and is a Fellow of the INAE, NASI, and AUTSE. He received the IIT Madras Lifetime Achievement Research Award (2021) and the Distinguished Alumnus Award of NIT Rourkela (2020).

Prof. Pulak Mohan Pandey

IHFC Chair & Professor (HAG), Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Delhi

Prof. Pulak Mohan Pandey is a distinguished academician and researcher in Additive Manufacturing, CAD/CAM, Biomedical Applications of 3D Printing, and Non-traditional Machining. He has served as the Director of BIET Jhansi (2021–2023) and has been a Professor at IIT Delhi since 2004. With a Ph.D. from IIT Kanpur, his contributions include pioneering research, patents, and impactful projects in advanced manufacturing.

Recognized with multiple Gandhian Young Technological Innovation Awards, the Outstanding Research Award (AMSI 2015), and the Distinguished Alumni Award (HBTU Kanpur), he continues to inspire students and researchers worldwide.

Prof. Uday Shanker Dixit

Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering &
Head, Center for Indian Knowledge System, IIT Guwahati

Prof. Uday Shanker Dixit is a distinguished academician and researcher in Modelling of Manufacturing Processes, Finite Element Analysis, Soft Computing, Design Optimization, and Design & Manufacturing of Mechatronic Systems. He also served as the Officiating Director of the Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar from February 2014 to May 2015.

Prof. Dixit has investigated numerous sponsored projects and developed several courses. He is a Fellow of the Indian Welding Society and serves as a National Advisory Committee Member of the International & All India Manufacturing, Technology and Design Conference. He has executed socially relevant projects, such as technology development to upgrade the skills of brass metal artisans of Hajo, Guwahati and the development of a machine for making plates out of areca nut. He has also actively mentored startups.

Dr. Sudip Dey

Associate Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering,
National Institute of Technology Silchar

Dr. Sudip Dey received his Bachelor in Mechanical Engineering (B.M.E) degree from Jadavpur University and completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Management. He earned his Ph.D. in Engineering from Jadavpur University. He has worked in reputed industries including Siemens, Garden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers Ltd., and Pipal Research Corporation.

He has extensive post-doctoral research and teaching experience from Swansea University, UK and Leibniz-Institut für Polymerforschung Dresden e. V., Germany. His name is enlisted in the World’s Top 2% Researchers & Scientists by Elsevier and Stanford University. His research spans from Classical Mechanics to Quantum Mechanics, and Innovation to Design, covering multi-disciplinary areas from Engineering to Applied Science.

He introduced the world’s first undergraduate elective course on Uncertainty Quantification (ME 483) at NIT Silchar. He has 4 patents and over 200 publications. He has led prestigious sponsored projects like SPARC and DRDO.
