Registration Fees

The registration fee covers participation in Technical Sessions, Conference kit, Lunch, and Tea/Coffee.

  • Indian students/scholars (Early bird) – Rs. 4000/-
  • Indian students/scholars (after 31/12/25) – Rs. 5000/-
  • International students/scholars (Early bird) – $100
  • International students/scholars (after 31/12/25) – $150
  • Indian academicians (Early bird) – Rs. 7000/-
  • Indian academicians (after 31/12/25) – Rs. 8000/-
  • International academicians (Early bird) – $200
  • International academicians (after 31/12/25) – $300
  • Industry participants from India (Early bird) – Rs. 10,000/-
  • Industry participants from India (after 31/12/25) – Rs. 11,000/-
  • Industry participants from outside India (Early bird) – $300
  • Industry participants from outside India (after 31/12/25) – $400

NOTE: Lodging is not included in the registration fees.
